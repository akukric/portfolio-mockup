Hipsters

By Harsh Patel

Overview

Hipster refers to a subculture of young, urban middle class adults and older teenagers that appeared in the 1990s. The subculture is associated with independent music, a varied non-mainstream fashion sensibility, progressive[1][2] or independent political views, alternative spirituality or atheism/agnosticisim and alternative lifestyles. Interests in media include independent film, magazines such as Clash, and websites like Pitchfork Media.
